About Leave Management System

The Leave Management System is a web-based application designed to streamline the leave application and approval process within organizations. It provides employees with a convenient platform to request leaves, view their leave balances, and track the status of their leave applications.

The purpose of the Leave Management System is to simplify and automate the leave management process, leading to increased efficiency, improved accuracy, and better employee satisfaction. The system offers several benefits, including:

  • Efficient leave request submission and approval
  • Accurate tracking of leave balances
  • Reduced paperwork and administrative burden
  • Improved transparency and communication
  • Customizable leave policies and configurations

Features:

  • Employee self-service portal for leave requests
  • Manager dashboard for leave approval
  • Automated leave balance calculations
  • Customizable leave types and policies
  • Notification system for leave status updates
